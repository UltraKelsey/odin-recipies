Kelsey's Pasta with Meat Sauce

Description

A simple, yet tasty meal that you can throw together on nights you want something quick & satisfying! Very forgiving - feel free to add roast veggies, substitute different kinds of meat, add any kind of fresh herb, etc!

Ingredients

  • Penne
  • Olive Oil
  • Italian Sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • Red Pasta Sauce
  • Basil
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • For Serving: Garlic Bread

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to package directions. Reserve a half cup of pasta water. Drain & set aside.
  2. Heat oil in pan. Chop onions. Add onions & sausage to pan until sausage is browned. Add minced garlic & oregano.
  3. Heat sauce in large pot until warmed through. Add cooked pasta, reserved pasta water, ground sausage & onions. Toss to combine.
  4. Top with parmesan, fresh basil & serve with garlic bread.
